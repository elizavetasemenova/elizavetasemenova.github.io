Hello! My name is Liza. I have not been updating this webpage since graduation from the PhD in 2019. For the most recent work, please see my Google Scholar or connect on LinkedIn.

Currectly I am a Research Associate in the Statistics section of the Department of Mathematics at Imperial College London. Before that I was a postdoctoral Fellow in Bayesian Machine Learning at AstraZeneca R&D (Cambridge, UK) and a PhD candidate in Epidemiology and Biostatistics at the Swiss TPH (Basel, Switzerland).

My interests lie on the intersection of novel model development and impactful real-life applications. On methodological side, they include Bayesian spatiotemporal modelling, Gaussian Processes, compartmental models (differential equations) and survey design. My main application fields are epidemiology, toxicology, and public policy.
